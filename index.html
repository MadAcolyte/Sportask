About App

Sportask is a mobile app that helps people to make new friends and and encourages people to do sports. It provides features such as tracking sports events near you, allowing you to join them or even create your own events.

Innovation

Accessability

Social Interaction

  • Lifetime map
    The lifetime map allows you to discover new sports activities in your area in real time.
  • Events
    Sportask's events feature allows users to browse and join sports events happening in their local area. We offer a diverse range of sports activities for you to choose from.
  • Challenges
    Sportask's challenge feature offers users the opportunity to set fitness goals. Whether it's a step challenge or a push-up challenge, users can track their progress and compare results with others in the group.
  • Android
    The app is available on Android devices, making it easily accessible to millions of users worldwide.
  • iOS
    We are working on this. You will be able to download Sportask on iOS soon.
  • Efficient Memory Usage
    One of the advantages of using Sportask is that the app requires only 85 MB of memory on your device.
  • Chat
    Sportask offers a chat and messaging feature that allows users to communicate, whether they are looking for workout partners or organising events.
  • Friends
    Users can add other users to their friend lists, which allows them to keep track of their connections and stay updated on their activities.
  • Groups
    Sportask Groups enable users to join communities of like-minded individuals who share similar interests or goals. Users can join groups based on their location, sport, or other factors.
